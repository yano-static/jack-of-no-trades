Jack of No Trades

1Growing up as a kid, I never had anyone to play with.2not even my family, as the youngest in a family of 7.3I was the loneliest kid out of my siblings.4while they go out with friends, party, drink, or play sports.5I was the kid who stayed at home, either crying alone or just playing alone.6Living in the AFP Headquarters from birth to 10 years old.7I spent almost my whole childhood in a Soldier's base.8Every birthday, and every celebration I spent at that place.9I find this place full of fear and emptied of love.10I didn't feel like a kid, more like an adult neglected of their emotions.11I was a Colonel's son.12not a lot of kids wanted to play with me because they were intimidated of me and my father.13I only played with one kid named Zaniel.14then he suddenly disappeared.15Then I was left alone again.16Suddenly my father wanted to retire early from the Army.17I was excited and scared at once.18I was excited I get to finally explore the world outside the Barracks that caged me.19Yet scared if I will be accepted by this said world.20finally, I'm out.

1Entering College life, full of scars, disbelief, distrust.2I wanted to go back to my childhood, where I just sit alone.3crying, playing games.4I never wanted to go through my teenage years, my graduating year ever again.5As I enter my Freshman year, still in UE.6I already had a name for myself.7I was the performer, The athlete, and the approachable.8I took Computer Science as my major.9and so far I'm having fun.10in my first year I met some friends along the way.11still have them now, but I'm still learning to trust them.12We went through lots of hardships together.13Academics, Emotional, Deep Talks, and other stuff.14I have everything already.15I have a good house, a complete, though not fun, family, friends.16But most of all, I'm a Jack of All--I'm a Jack of No Trades.17I might know everything, but it doesn't amount to knowledge or physicality.18that can reach to somewhere succesful.19I know everything, yet know nothing.20This is the biography of Vincent Palanas, The Jack of No Trades.
